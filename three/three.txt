// ho to use create github account

simple steps:

Go to https://github.com and click on "Sign up" or "Join GitHub".
Fill out the registration form with your email address, password, and username.
Verify your email address by clicking on the link sent to you by GitHub.
You can then set up your profile by adding a profile picture, bio, and other information.
After creating your account, you can start exploring GitHub and creating your own repositories. You can also search for open-source projects to contribute to or learn from.

Signing up for a new personal account

Navigate to https://github.com.
Click Sign up.
Follow the prompts to create your personal account. [1]
